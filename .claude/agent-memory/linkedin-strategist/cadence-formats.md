**Validated cadence for Leilaine Campioto (and applicable to similar executive B2B clients):**

## 3x/Week Rotation

| Day | Format | Pillar type | Time | Primary goal |
|-----|--------|-------------|------|--------------|
| Monday | Long narrative (1300-2500 chars) | Process/leadership | 8h15 | Authority + dwell time + comments |
| Wednesday | Carousel (document) or article | AI/education | 8h00 | Distribution + saves + profile visits |
| Friday | Medium confrontation (400-900 chars) | Emotional/faith/purpose | 8h00 | Engagement + shares |

**Why 8h-9h window:** Construção civil proprietários and directors start early. LinkedIn consumption peak for this audience is 7h30-9h00 before first meeting of the day. Do not post after 10h for this audience.

## Format Rules

**Long narrative post:**
- First 2 lines must stand alone before "ver mais" — complete thought or provocative question
- Use --- dividers to break into sections (LinkedIn renders them as visual breathing room)
- Never end with "Concorda?" or "O que acha?" — end with an open diagnostic question or a statement that invites agreement/disagreement

**Carousel (document):**
- Cover slide = the uncomfortable truth, not a title
- Slide 2 = name the problem specifically (why the uncomfortable truth is real)
- Slides 3-8 = specific points, each self-contained (reader may stop at any slide)
- Slide 9 = the reframe / payoff
- Slide 10 = CTA with link instruction pointing to first comment
- Link NEVER in body of post — always first comment

**Short confrontation:**
- Max 3 paragraphs
- Each paragraph separated by blank line
- No hashtags on confrontation posts — they read as editorial, not promotional
- No link in first comment — pure engagement play

## Algorithm Notes (2026)

- Dwell time > reaction count. A post that people read for 45 seconds with 8 meaningful comments outperforms a post with 200 likes and 3 one-word comments.
- First 60 minutes determine distribution. First comment (with link if applicable) should go live within 5-10 minutes of posting.
- Editing within first 10 min: neutral. Editing after 10 min: mild penalty on text posts.
- External links in post body: penalized. Always drop in first comment.
- Native video (uploaded directly): strong distribution. YouTube links: penalized.

**How to apply:** For any new client, load this cadence as default. Adjust pillar assignment per client's content strategy but keep the Mon/Wed/Fri timing and format rotation.
