Máquina de Vendas course redesigned from 4 basic tutorial lessons to 5 modules / 15 lessons with full briefings.

**Why:** Original course was a 4-step technical tutorial with no market context, no business model teaching, no progression to Hub. Moroni wanted more context than how-to, plus revenue models, prospecting, multi-agents, and Hub as final destination.

**How to apply:** All briefings are in `brain/assets/content/maquina-de-vendas-curriculum.md`. Next steps: scriptwriter creates roteiros from briefings, educational designer creates visuals, then director reviews. Pipeline status board is at the bottom of the file. The course balances ~60% context/strategy and ~40% technical content. Hub is mentioned only 3 times (1.1 seed, 4.3 context, 5.2 full presentation) to avoid being pushy.

Key decisions:
- Assessoria positioned as best revenue model (not forced, but argued)
- Student builds agent from scratch BEFORE seeing Cloudfy template (competence over convenience)
- Multi-agents taught at concept/design level only — implementation is Hub/Builders territory
- 5 complementary PDFs suggested as additional materials
