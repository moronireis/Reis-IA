# Creative Patterns — Brazilian Wedding Market (April 2026)

## Hooks That Work
1. "Você sabe quanto custa um casamento em [year]?" — curiosity + financial fear
2. "X erros que noivas cometem na hora de contratar assessoria" — listicle fear hook
3. "Como [bride name] casou em [X meses] sem estresse" — social proof narrative
4. Pain-first: "Você está perdida no planejamento do seu casamento?" — direct identification

## Angles That Are SATURATED (avoid)
- "Sem estresse" — owned by Noiva Sem Estresse brand
- Generic checklists as lead magnets — every player does this
- "X anos de experiência" — commoditized credibility
- "Realizamos seu sonho" — zero specificity, maximum genericness

## Formats That Are UNDERUSED (opportunity)
- Video Reels showing the planning process in real time (behind the scenes)
- Carousel: "7 perguntas que você deve fazer antes de contratar uma assessoria"
- Before/after: client testimonial with specific outcome (budget saved, stress reduced)
- UGC-style: bride talking to camera on day-of

## CTAs Observed
- "Fale conosco no WhatsApp" (dominant — 80%+ of market)
- "Solicite seu orçamento" (generic)
- "Agende sua consulta gratuita" (occasional)
- ZERO use of quiz CTAs despite this being high-intent lead qualification

## Noiva S.A. Differentiation Angle
The S.A. (Sociedade Anônima) positioning is unique — corporate/business framing of wedding planning is UNUSED by any competitor. Lean into: professionalism, system, method, accountability — not romance and dreams.
