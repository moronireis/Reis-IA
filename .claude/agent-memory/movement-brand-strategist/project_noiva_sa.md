Client project: Noiva S.A. — wedding ecosystem brand led by Giovanna Paola.

**Movement delivered:** `noiva-sa-platform/brandbook/03-movement-architecture.md` (v1.0, 2026-04-08)

Key decisions:
- "S.A." reinterpreted as "Sociedade do Amor" (not Sociedade Anonima) — woven throughout
- Cause: "Casamento nao e industria. E a celebracao mais importante da vida de alguem."
- External enemy: "Sistema do Casamento-Producao" (buffets as symptom, commoditization as root)
- Internal enemy: "A Solidao da Decisao" (groom not understanding, fear of spending wrong, assessor self-doubt, vendor marketing paralysis)
- Vocabulary: 20 wedding-metaphor terms (Socia, O Sim, Mesa Posta, Prova do Vestido, Alianca, Lua de Mel, etc.)
- 4 commitment levels: Curiosa → Convidada → Socia → Socia Master → Circulo Interno
- Manifesto written in PT-BR, emotionally grounded in Giovanna's triple origin story
- 10% humanitarian tithe woven into cause narrative
- Design system alignment: Rose=#DBA99F (amor), Eucalipto=#4A6741 (crescimento), Taupe=#9C958F (estrutura)
- Symbol: Alianca Entrecruzada (two interlocking circles)

**Why:** This is a CLIENT project, not internal REIS [IA] brand work. Different audience, different tone, different design system.
**How to apply:** When working on Noiva S.A., load this context. Do not mix with Builder movement vocabulary or REIS [IA] brand identity.
