Client project: Lideres Leoes — leadership strengthening movement led by Leilaine Campioto (ZNITH brand).

**Movement delivered:** `znith-brand/branding/movement-lideres-leoes-concept.md` (v1.0, 2026-04-08)

Key decisions:
- Cause: "Leadership is governance, not performance. Leaders called to carry the most weight are the ones most abandoned by the systems that should form them."
- Primary slogan: "Coragem para governar. Consciencia para permanecer."
- Call-and-response: "Quem governa? Quem permanece."
- External villain: Performance-Leadership Industrial Complex (4 faces: Charisma Factory, Formula Merchants, Hype Machine, Shortcut Sellers)
- Internal villain: The Disconnection — 5 patterns (Imposter Governor, Exhausted Performer, Conflict Avoider, Identity Fracture, Self-Deceiver)
- 6 commitment levels: Outsider → Observador → Participante → Membro → Selo de Governo → Lider
- Advancement is RECOGNIZED by tribe, never applied for — prevents ambition from corrupting the system
- 20+ vocabulary terms centered on governance language (Governo, Consciencia, Direcao, Postura, Territorio, Peso, Alinhamento, Fortalecimento)
- Entry ritual: Posture Declaration (3 questions, must be spoken aloud)
- Weekly: Governance Circle. Monthly: O Confronto. Annual: O Acampamento.
- Lion symbol: watchful/vigilant rest posture, NOT aggressive/roaring
- Colors: Gold (legacy/authority), Deep Navy (depth/maturity), Black (governance weight), White (truth/clarity)
- 24-month target: 100 active, committed leaders with visible governance transformation
- Critical constraints: NOT motivational community, NOT spiritualized networking, must maintain spiritual base without losing depth for commercial palatability
- Faith-strategy integration is core — no separation between spiritual and professional identity

**Why:** Client project, not REIS [IA] brand. Entirely different audience (faith-based leaders vs digital professionals), different tone (confrontational truth vs builder energy), different design system (gold+navy vs electric blue).
**How to apply:** When working on Lideres Leoes / ZNITH, load this context. Do not mix with Builder movement or REIS [IA] identity.
