## Brazil AI Market (2026)
- US$3.4B investment in AI software/services/infrastructure (IDC)
- 41.9% of industrial companies use AI — was 16.9% in 2022 (IBGE/PINTEC)
- 61% of Brazilian SMBs use some AI tool (Serasa Experian)
- 52% of small entrepreneurs consider AI essential to compete (InfinitePay)
- 95% of companies that adopted AI report revenue growth, avg 31% increase
