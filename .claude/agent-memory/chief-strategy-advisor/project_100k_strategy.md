Reis IA strategic analysis completed 2026-03-25 with recommendation for "Sell-Build-Scale" hybrid approach.

**Why:** Business is at early growth stage with strong strategic infrastructure (positioning, brand, copy, websites) but zero documented revenue or case studies. The gap is execution, not strategy.

**How to apply:**
- Phase 1 (weeks 1-4): Pure sales focus — outreach, event conversion (MdV 26/03), close Builder early birds + Systems starters
- Phase 2 (weeks 5-8): Build funnel + content engine while continuing sales
- Phase 3 (weeks 9-16): Scale with proof — case studies, Partners, ads, second cohort
- Revenue math: 8 Builder clients × R$7.5K + 4 Systems × R$6K + 10 Audits × R$1.5K = R$104K
- Key metric to track: sales conversations per week
- Primary risk: dispersão (too many fronts, not enough selling)
- Viver de IA is the benchmark competitor — R$100M projected 2026, 1200+ companies, R$3.5K/mo subscription model
- Brazil AI market: US$3.4B in 2026, 41.9% of industrial companies using AI (IBGE), 61% of SMBs using some AI tool
