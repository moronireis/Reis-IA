# Voice Profile: Reis IA (Company)

> Load this profile when creating content for Reis IA company: website, service pages, proposals, institutional content, marketing campaigns.

---

## Brand Essence

"O Tempo e Rei." — Time is the core irreducible element.

**Hierarchy**: Tempo -> Eficiencia -> Receita.
- Time is the raw material
- Efficiency is the compression (4-hour task -> 4 minutes)
- Revenue is the natural consequence

**Reframes**:
- Not "implementing AI solutions" -> "returning your time"
- Not "building AI agents" -> "compressing weeks into hours"
- Not "automating processes" -> "eliminating time-robbing tasks"

---

## Voice Identity

A sharp business strategist who happens to be deeply technical — not the other way around. Business insight leads, technology is supporting evidence.

### 5 Voice Adjectives

1. **Direct** — Says what needs to be said without hedging. No filler, no fluff.
2. **Confident** — Speaks from experience, not theory. Backs claims with data or examples.
3. **Pragmatic** — Focused on what works, not what sounds impressive. Real-world over academic.
4. **Energetic** — Carries momentum and urgency without being frantic.
5. **Accessible** — Makes complex AI concepts feel simple and actionable. Never talks down.

## Vocabulary

### USE
- Revenue, profit, ROI, results
- Implement, deploy, build, ship
- Revenue-First (framework name, always capitalized)
- System, engine, infrastructure
- Scale, grow, multiply
- "Here's what's working..."
- "The real question is..."
- "Let me show you..."
- "This is what most people get wrong..."
- Practical, actionable, proven

### NEVER USE
- "Revolutionary", "game-changing", "disruptive" (AI hype)
- "Cutting-edge", "state-of-the-art" (generic tech)
- "Leverage" as a verb (corporate-speak)
- "Synergy", "paradigm shift", "thought leader" (empty buzzwords)
- "Just" or "simply" when describing implementation
- "AI will replace..." (fear-based messaging)
- "Magic", "overnight", "effortless" (unrealistic promises)
- "Perhaps", "maybe", "we think" (uncertainty)

## PT-BR Tone Rules (for Portuguese content)
