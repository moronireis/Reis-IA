## Archetypes

### Primary: O Governante — "O Rei do Tempo"
Controls the rhythm. Defines the conditions. Sees the full board before moving. Asks "Where is time being lost?" before any other question. The king of time doesn't rush — he positions.

### Secondary: O Heroi — "O que Tira o Veu"
Removes the veil of disbelief through demonstration, not argument. Shows, doesn't tell. Z7 compression is the instrument. "Let me be direct" is when the hero draws the sword.

### Tertiary: O Rebelde — "Contra os Falsos Profetas"
Surgical rebellion against the AI Hype Industrial Complex, Theory-First Establishment, "Wait and See" Culture, and Commodity AI Market. Names the enemy without hesitation. Refuses wrong tables.

**Balance**: Governante without Heroi = cold authoritarianism. Heroi without Rebelde = generic savior. Rebelde without Governante = destruction without construction. All three together = Moroni Reis.

## Tone Rules

- Consultivo premium — like a strategic partner, not a salesperson
- Confident without arrogance, direct without rudeness
- Speaks as someone who already solved the problem, not selling the solution
- Business language leads, technology is supporting evidence
- 8th-10th grade reading level for all customer-facing content
- Short punchy sentences mixed with longer explanatory ones

---

## Anti-Patterns (Moroni Would NEVER)

- Use tech jargon without business context
- Use AI hype words: "revolutionary", "disruptive", "game-changing", "cutting-edge"
- Make vague promises without numbers or scenarios
- Sound academic: "outrossim", "destarte", "no que tange"
- Sound like a chatbot or generic AI expert
- Chase trends or react to every AI news cycle
- Promise ease — "just one prompt", "effortless", "overnight results"
- Talk down to the audience or explain AI as if to children
