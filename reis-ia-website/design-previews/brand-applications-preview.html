Guidelines
Brand Misuse Examples
These examples show incorrect usage of the Reis IA mark. Each violation degrades brand integrity and must be avoided in all applications.
Stretched / Distorted
Never alter the aspect ratio. The mark must always maintain its original proportions.
Unauthorized Colors
Only white (#FFF), accent blue (#4A90FF), or black (#000). No orange, green, red, or other hues.
Below Minimum Size
Never render smaller than 16px. Below this threshold the open intersection gap becomes illegible.
Complex Background
The mark requires clean, uncluttered backgrounds. Never place over photos, patterns, or gradients.
Rotated
The mark must always appear upright at 0 degrees. No tilting, rotating, or angled placement.
Distorting Effects
No drop shadows, outer glows, 3D bevels, or embossing. The only permitted effect is the ambient glow in hero contexts.
