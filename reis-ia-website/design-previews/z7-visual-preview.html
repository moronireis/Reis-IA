Section 1

Z7 Construction

The Z7 mark is built from a fundamental geometric insight: the number 7 and the letter Z, when overlaid, form the silhouette of an hourglass. The 7 provides the upper triangle (top bar + descending diagonal). The Z provides the lower triangle (ascending diagonal + bottom bar). Where their diagonals cross at the center, the hourglass waist emerges.
