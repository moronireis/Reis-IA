Section 01

Design Philosophy

Five principles govern every visual decision, ordered by priority.

01
Restraint Is the Design
Every element must earn its space. Black space is the primary premium signal.
02
Typography Is Architecture
Type does 90% of the hierarchy work. Dense impact vs open legibility.
03
Dark Mode Mastery
Layered surface system. Depth through background shifts, not shadows.
04
Conversion Through Clarity
Blue accent exists for conversion. Max 2 blue elements per viewport.
05
Engineered Precision
Every value intentional. Custom easings, fluid clamp(), optical tuning.
