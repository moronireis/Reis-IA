Signature Elements

The 3-4 visual treatments that define Academia Lendaria's design personality, with adaptation notes for Reis IA.

1. Rotating Conic Gradient Borders

The most distinctive visual element. A subtle bright spot rotates around card edges using animated CSS custom properties. Creates a premium, tech-forward feel without being flashy.

Original (gray)
Reis IA (gold)
Reis IA Adaptation
Replace the gray gradient highlights (#646464) with Reis IA gold (#C9A84C / #D4B65E). Slow the rotation to 8s for added sophistication. Use on pricing cards or the "How It Works" process section. Limit to 2-3 cards per page to maintain impact.

2. 3D Perspective Scroll Entrance

Content enters with combined translateY + rotateX creating a "cards flipping up from a table" effect. More sophisticated than standard fade-ups. The 1.2s duration with refined cubic-bezier adds weight and gravitas.

3D Perspective Entrance
Reis IA Adaptation
Use selectively -- hero section and one key content section only. Over-use feels gimmicky. Keep the slow 1.2s timing; do not speed it up. The cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.46, 0.45, 0.94) easing is critical to the premium feel. Consider using for the "Revenue-First Framework" reveal.

3. Slow Text Shimmer

Headlines use background-clip: text with a gradient that creates an almost imperceptible shimmer. The 13-second cycle is deliberately slow -- you notice it subconsciously rather than consciously.

Original (white-gray)
Revenue-First AI
Reis IA Adaptation (white-gold)
Revenue-First AI
Reis IA Adaptation
Replace the gray shimmer (#b8b8b8) with Reis IA gold (#C9A84C / #D4B65E). Extend the cycle to 15s for even more subtlety. Use only on the hero headline of each page. The gold shimmer reinforces brand color without being obvious.

4. Shadow-Free Depth System

Depth is created entirely through background color layering and border treatments, not box-shadows. This is unusual and distinctive -- most dark-mode sites use subtle shadows for elevation. The result is a flatter, more architectural feel.

Level 0 #000
Level 1 #0a0a0a
Level 2 #111
Level 3 #141414
Level 4 #161616
Level 5 #1a1a1a
Border #242424
Reis IA Adaptation
Directly adopt this layered depth system. Reis IA already uses bg-black / bg-neutral-950 alternation. Extend to include #141414 and #1a1a1a for card surfaces. Continue avoiding box-shadows on dark backgrounds -- they look artificial. Use borders (#242424) and background differentiation for all elevation.
