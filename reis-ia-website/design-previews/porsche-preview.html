04

3 Easing Curves

No bounce, no elastic, no spring physics. Porsche motion is mechanical precision. Click each card to see the easing in action.

Animated Comparisons

Base

cubic-bezier(0.25, 0.1, 0.25, 1)
Fast start, even deceleration, smooth settle. All-purpose.

In (Exit)

cubic-bezier(0, 0, 0.2, 1)
Linear start, gradual acceleration. For elements leaving.

Out (Enter)

cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.5, 1)
Gentle start, controlled arrival. For elements entering.
05

Typography

3 font weights (400, 600, 700), fluid type scale with clamp(), and a calculated line-height formula: calc(6px + 2.125ex).

Line Height Formula

calc(6px + 2.125ex) at different sizes

The formula produces optically-correct spacing at every size. At 16px it yields ~1.5 ratio, at 48px ~1.17 ratio, at 80px ~1.1 ratio.
