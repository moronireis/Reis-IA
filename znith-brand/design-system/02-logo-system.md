# ZNITH — Logo System & Visual Identity
**Version:** 1.0
**Last updated:** April 2026
**Owner:** Leilaine Campioto / ZNITH Brand

---

## Overview

The ZNITH brand identity is built around three visual elements that work as a system: the **Master Mark** (standing lion with crown and wordmark), the **Reduced Mark** (stylized Z with integrated lion face), and the **Wordmark** ("ZNITH" in Cinzel serif). Together they express the brand's core positioning — a premium commercial expansion consultancy with AI — and its guiding philosophy: Direção. Consciência. Legado.

All logo assets are located in: `../source/logos/`

## 1. Logo Principal (Master Mark)

### Composição

The full ZNITH logo is a stacked composition of three elements:

1. **Símbolo** — A standing lion rendered in a dark ink illustration style, wearing a golden crown. The lion faces forward with presence and authority.
2. **Wordmark** — "ZNITH" in Cinzel serif, set below the symbol.
3. **Tagline** — "DIREÇÃO. CONSCIÊNCIA. LEGADO." in Montserrat, set below the wordmark in a wide-tracked uppercase treatment.

### Versões de Layout

#### Versão Principal — Vertical (Stacked)

The default version. Symbol above, wordmark below, tagline below wordmark. All elements are center-aligned.

- **Com tagline:** `../source/logos/ZINITH_oficial_png.png`
- **Sem tagline:** `../source/logos/ZINITH_oficial_png - sem frase.png`

Use this version as the default in all primary applications: website headers, presentations, proposals, printed materials, signage.

The tagline version is preferred when space permits and brand context is being established for the first time (first page of a document, website hero, brand introduction). The version without tagline is preferred for repeated applications within the same document, embossing, or space-constrained headers.

#### Versão Secundária — Horizontal

The lion symbol sits on the left. The wordmark and tagline stack vertically on the right, left-aligned to the symbol's right edge.

- **Arquivo:** `../source/logos/ZINITH_horizontal_png.png`

Use this version in landscape-constrained contexts: email headers, document headers, presentation slide headers, co-branded materials, wide banners.

## 3. Wordmark

### Especificações Tipográficas

- **Fonte:** Cinzel
- **Peso:** Regular (400) or SemiBold (600) — SemiBold preferred for primary applications
- **Case:** All caps — "ZNITH"
- **Letter-spacing:** +0.08em to +0.12em (tight but not compressed — the natural Cinzel spacing is appropriate; avoid collapsing further)
- **Caracteres:** Z · N · I · T · H

Cinzel is a serif typeface designed to evoke classical Roman stone carving — its proportions and stroke contrast reinforce the brand's authority and legacy positioning.

### Uso Isolado do Wordmark

The wordmark "ZNITH" may be used without the lion symbol only in contexts where:

1. The full logo has already appeared on the same surface (e.g., the cover of a document already featuring the master mark)
2. Space is severely constrained and the reduced mark (Z) is not appropriate
3. The context calls for inline text treatment (e.g., within a sentence or tagline in a layout)

Never use the wordmark alone as the primary logo on first-impression materials (proposals, website hero, presentations).

### Tamanho Mínimo do Wordmark

| Contexto | Tamanho Mínimo |
|---|---|
| Digital | 80 px de largura |
| Impresso | 25 mm de largura |

## 4. Tagline

### Tagline Principal

**"Direção. Consciência. Legado."**

Three words. Each a complete statement. The period after each word is intentional and must not be removed — it creates rhythm and finality.

### Tagline Secundária

**"Porque IA sem direção só acelera o caos."**

Used in campaign contexts, presentation sections, and long-form copy where the brand positioning needs to be stated with strategic edge. Not a logo element — a copy/messaging element deployed in layouts.

### Especificações Tipográficas

- **Fonte:** Montserrat
- **Peso:** Medium (500) or SemiBold (600)
- **Case:** Uppercase — "DIREÇÃO. CONSCIÊNCIA. LEGADO."
- **Letter-spacing:** +0.15em to +0.25em — wide, breathable tracking
- **Color on dark backgrounds:** Gold — #DF9F3E
- **Color on light backgrounds:** Dark navy — #091022

### Posicionamento em Relação ao Logo

In the vertical (stacked) logo composition:

```
        [LION SYMBOL]
           ZNITH
  DIREÇÃO. CONSCIÊNCIA. LEGADO.
```

- Spacing between wordmark and tagline: equal to the cap-height of the wordmark text
- The tagline is never wider than the wordmark above it — if it exceeds the wordmark width at a given size, reduce letter-spacing proportionally

In the horizontal logo composition:

```
[LION]  ZNITH
        DIREÇÃO. CONSCIÊNCIA. LEGADO.
```

- The tagline sits directly below the wordmark, left-aligned to it
- Tagline font size is approximately 40–50% of the wordmark font size

### Uso Independente da Tagline

The tagline may appear independently in layouts, slide backgrounds, and section dividers as a typographic element. In independent use, it retains its uppercase Montserrat treatment and wide tracking. It should not be placed inside the logo clear-space zone.

## 6. Área de Proteção (Clear Space)

Clear space is the minimum zone of visual isolation around the logo. No other graphic elements, text, images, or edges of the page may enter this zone.

### Unidade de Medida

The clear space unit is defined as **the cap-height of the letter "Z" in the ZNITH wordmark** at any given reproduction size. This is referred to as "1x" below.

### Logo Vertical (Stacked)

```
         1x
    ┌─────────────┐
 1x │             │ 1x
    │  [SYMBOL]   │
    │   ZNITH     │
    │  [tagline]  │
 1x │             │
    └─────────────┘
         1x
```

Minimum 1x clear space on all four sides of the logo bounding box. The bounding box extends from the outermost edge of the lion illustration at the top to the baseline of the tagline text at the bottom, and from the leftmost to rightmost extent of any element.

### Logo Horizontal

```
              1x
    ┌──────────────────────┐
 1x │  [lion] ZNITH        │ 1x
    │         [tagline]    │
 1x │                      │
    └──────────────────────┘
              1x
```

Same rule applies — 1x on all sides.

### Símbolo Isolado (Z mark)

```
      1x
  ┌───────┐
1x│  [Z]  │1x
  └───────┘
      1x
```

Minimum 1x clear space. For the Z mark, 1x = the height of the Z letterform itself.

## 8. Uso Incorreto

The following applications violate the ZNITH logo system and must not be produced or approved.

1. **Não distorcer ou esticar.** Never stretch the logo horizontally or vertically. The proportions of the lion illustration and wordmark are fixed. Maintain aspect ratio when scaling.

2. **Não usar cores fora da paleta da marca.** The logo appears in navy (#091022), white (#FFFFFF), and gold (#DF9F3E, #FFD161) only. Do not recolor the lion red, green, purple, or any other color.

3. **Não adicionar efeitos.** No drop shadows, inner shadows, glow effects, gradients applied to the logo, emboss, bevel, or 3D extrusion. The lion illustration already carries depth — do not add artificial effects on top.

4. **Não colocar sobre fundos sem contraste.** Do not place the logo directly on busy photography, patterned backgrounds, or mid-tone backgrounds (e.g., medium gray) where neither the dark nor light version reads cleanly. Apply a solid background panel or use a photo overlay (see Section 9).

5. **Não rotacionar.** The logo always appears at 0° rotation. No angled, flipped, or mirrored versions.

6. **Não separar o leão do wordmark em contextos de logo principal.** The lion illustration is not a standalone graphic — it functions as part of the master mark. Only the designated reduced mark (Z.png, Z-2.png, Z-3.png) may be used as an isolated symbol.

7. **Não usar versões de baixa resolução.** Never upscale rasterized PNG assets. At the required display size, the logo must be crisp. For large-format print (A2 and above), request vector source files (SVG/AI/EPS).

8. **Não modificar a coroa.** The crown on the lion's head is part of the illustration and must not be removed, repositioned, resized independently, or replaced with a different crown graphic.

9. **Não alterar a tipografia do wordmark.** "ZNITH" always appears in Cinzel. Never substitute another font, even temporarily. Never hand-letter or recreate the wordmark.

10. **Não adicionar texto dentro da área de proteção.** No taglines, slogans, URLs, phone numbers, or any other text elements may be placed within the logo's clear-space zone. All supporting information must appear outside the 1x buffer.

11. **Não criar combinações não autorizadas.** Do not combine the grafismo elements with the main logo in ways not documented here. The grafismos are supporting elements — they do not replace or frame the master mark.

12. **Não usar o Grafismo6 (coroa 3D) como elemento de marca recorrente** without explicit campaign brief approval (see Section 5).

### Fundos Permitidos (Com Cuidado)

| Fundo | Condição |
|---|---|
| Branco sólido (#FFFFFF) | Usar versão navy do logo — leão em tinta escura, wordmark em navy |
| Cinza claro | Testar contraste — wordmark deve ter relação mínima de contraste 4.5:1 |
| Cores sólidas escuras (verde escuro, bordô, etc.) | Permitido em contextos de co-branding ou campanha específica com aprovação |

### Fundos Proibidos

| Tipo | Razão |
|---|---|
| Gradientes como fundo direto | O logo precisa de um campo de cor estável |
| Fotografias sem overlay | A textura e os tons variáveis da foto competem com a ilustração do leão |
| Padrões gráficos (incluindo os próprios grafismos ZNITH) | Legibilidade comprometida |
| Fundos em tons médios (cinza médio, azul médio) | Nem o logo claro nem o escuro leem com clareza suficiente |

### Regra de Overlay em Fotografia

When the ZNITH logo must appear over a photograph (event backdrops, social media, presentations):

- Apply a solid dark overlay of **minimum 60% opacity** in navy (#091022) or black over the photo
- The logo then sits on this overlay field — not directly on the photo
- Alternatively, use a solid color panel (navy or black) behind the logo within the photo composition

## 10. Co-branding

When ZNITH appears alongside partner, client, or sponsor logos:

### Princípios Gerais

- ZNITH and the partner logo must be separated by a visible dividing element — either a vertical rule line or a minimum gap of **2x** (two times the clear-space unit defined in Section 6)
- ZNITH logo and partner logo should appear at **comparable visual weight** — neither dominating the other disproportionately
- If ZNITH is the primary brand in the context (e.g., ZNITH's own materials referencing a partner), the ZNITH logo may be slightly larger — no more than 130% of the partner logo's height
- If the partner is the primary brand (e.g., a partner's event), the ZNITH logo is the secondary element and appears at equal or smaller size

### Regras de Espaçamento

```
   [ZNITH LOGO]   |   [PARTNER LOGO]
         ↑ 2x gap or rule line ↑
```

The 2x spacing is measured from the outermost edge of each logo's bounding box to the center of the dividing rule (or to the midpoint of the gap).

### Background em Co-branding

- Prefer a neutral background (white or navy) that accommodates both logos cleanly
- If the partner's brand uses a different color background, negotiate a shared neutral field for the co-branded lockup
- Do not place the ZNITH logo on a background color that belongs to the partner's brand without approval

### O Que Não Fazer em Co-branding

- Do not merge or overlap the ZNITH lion with any partner mark
- Do not use the ZNITH reduced mark (Z) alongside a partner's full logo — match complexity (full logo with full logo, or mark with mark)
- Do not allow a partner logo to appear inside ZNITH's clear-space zone
