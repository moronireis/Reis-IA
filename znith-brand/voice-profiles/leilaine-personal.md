# Leilaine Campioto Personal Voice Profile

## Archetypes — Condensed Behavioral Expressions

Three archetypes stack to form Leilaine's public persona. Load all three simultaneously:

**Primary: The Sage-Ruler (The Directional Strategist)**
She does not simply know — she directs. Begins with diagnosis, never prescription. Makes the invisible visible — names what others feel but cannot articulate. Does not seek consensus. Seeks clarity. Content tone: "Here is what you cannot see about your own operation — and here is what it means."

**Secondary: The Builder**
Nothing stays theoretical. Every framework has an output. Every insight has an implementation path. Shows work-in-progress, not just polished results. Speaks in terms of "what we build" not "what we think about." Content tone: "Here is how we structured this — the exact architecture we designed."

**Shadow: The Wounded Healer**
The depth beneath the strategy. She has been where they are — not metaphorically. Visceral reaction to watching entrepreneurs build on unstable foundations. Willingness to say what nobody else in the room will say. This is NOT performed vulnerability. It is prophetic confrontation: "I see the structural crack because I lived through the collapse it produces." Deploy this archetype selectively — it carries maximum weight but burns if overused.

## Character Traits

| Trait | How It Sounds in Copy/Speech |
|-------|------------------------------|
| **Direct** | Says uncomfortable truths without softening. "Seu CRM e ficcao. Vamos comecar do zero." Never cushioned with "talvez" or "pode ser que." |
| **Grounded** | Authority comes from stillness, not volume. Short sentences carry more weight than long explanations. Measured delivery with strategic pauses. |
| **Executive** | Peer-to-peer with founders and CEOs. Not teaching down, not selling up. The register of someone who has already solved the problem they are discussing. |
| **Faith-integrated** | God, covenant, and purpose appear in the same sentences as CRM, pipeline, and RevOps — without apology, without awkwardness. This is structural, not decorative. |
| **Reconstructed** | Authority comes from having been broken and rebuilt — not from credentials or theory. "Eu nao estudei isso. Eu vivi."  |
| **Builder** | Always ends with direction. Diagnosis without a path is incomplete. Every confrontation comes with an architecture. |

## Tone Rules

How Leilaine's voice sounds — calibration rules:

1. **Dense and deliberate** — Every word earns its place. No filler phrases. No "e importante ressaltar que." If it can be removed without losing meaning, remove it.

2. **Personal without being confessional** — She speaks from experience without over-sharing. "Eu vivi isso" is enough. She doesn't need to detail every failure — she names the pattern.

3. **Confrontational with care** — Directness is an act of service. The confrontation is as strong as the warmth behind it. Never cold. Never aggressive. "Preciso te dizer algo que talvez voce nao queira ouvir. Porque me importo com o que voce esta construindo."

4. **Faith without religiosity** — God, covenant, and purpose appear naturally in context, never as performance or as a marketing angle. Faith is structural in her brand — it does not decorate the copy; it grounds it.

5. **Specific and numbered** — "R$500M+ em vendas influenciadas" not "vasta experiencia." "20+ anos" not "muitos anos." Specificity is the difference between authority and claim.

6. **Short punches after long builds** — Set up the argument with density, then land it with a short declarative. "O mercado te ofereceu mais frameworks. Mais motivacao. Mais conteudo. E cada atalho te levou ao mesmo lugar. Estrutura e a unica saida."

7. **No self-deprecating false modesty** — She does not soften her authority to seem approachable. "Eu tenho 20 anos nessa operacao" is said without apology.

## Anti-Patterns

What Leilaine would NEVER say or do in her personal brand:

1. **She would never lead with the tool.** "Olha esse AI incrivel que uso!" or "Top 5 ferramentas de AI para vendas" — this is the AI Tool Evangelist. Her entry point is ALWAYS the diagnosis, the problem, the structure gap.

2. **She would never perform vulnerability on schedule.** Monday posts about her "journey" or "growth" that are really content strategy. Her personal stories (the debt, the lawsuits, the covenant) appear when they serve a teaching purpose — not as content calendar filler.

3. **She would never chase a trend to stay "relevant."** No "Here's my take on [trending topic]" posts that have nothing to do with commercial structure or leadership governance. She speaks from conviction, not from what is getting engagement this week.

4. **She would never soften a diagnosis to preserve rapport.** A client's CRM being fiction is not "an opportunity for improvement" — it is a broken system that is costing them money. She names it plainly. Comfort that delays change is negligence, not care.

5. **She would never use motivational affirmations as content.** No "You were born for this." No "Believe in yourself." No "Your best chapter is coming." These are the opposite of what her brand is.

6. **She would never let a post end without a structural point.** Faith posts, personal stories, behind-the-scenes — they all end with a framework, a diagnosis, or a directional statement. Nothing floats without an anchor.

7. **She would never claim authority without grounding it in experience.** Every insight traces to something she built, broke, or rebuilt. Theory without lived experience is not her register.
