# ZNITH Company Voice Profile

## Brand Essence

One irreducible sentence: **ZNITH architects commercial operations so that companies grow with direction, consciousness, and permanence.**

The word ZNITH owns: **architecture** — not as metaphor, but as discipline. Deliberate, diagnostic-first design of commercial systems that produce predictable growth.

---

## Voice Identity

Five adjectives that govern every word written in the ZNITH master brand voice:

| Adjective | What it means in practice |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Consultive** | Speaks from a position of having solved the problem before. Never from a sales posture, never from a teaching posture. Peer-to-peer with founders and commercial directors. |
| **Direct** | Sentences have no ornament. One statement. One point. No hedging, no softening. If something is broken, say it is broken. |
| **Architectural** | Thinks and communicates in systems. Every recommendation has a structural reason. Every observation leads to a diagnosis. Always asks "what is the underlying structure causing this?" |
| **Accountable** | Never delivers and disappears. Tracks outcomes, not deliverables. Speaks in commercial results: revenue, pipeline, conversion rates — not process activity. |
| **Mature** | Does not chase trends. Does not react to market noise. Makes decisions from conviction, not anxiety. AI is infrastructure — not a trend to follow or a stage prop. |

## Tone Spectrum

Tone shifts by channel. Core identity stays constant.

| Channel | Tone | Register | Example opener |
|---------|------|----------|----------------|
| Website | Clear, authoritative, outcome-focused | Short declarative statements, no preamble | "Empresas que crescem com previsibilidade nao tiveram sorte. Tiveram arquitetura." |
| LinkedIn (institutional) | Confident, provocative, conversation-starting | Question or provocative assertion leads | "A maioria das empresas nao tem problema de venda. Tem problema de estrutura comercial." |
| Proposals | Professional, data-driven, specific | Dense paragraphs, specific numbers, structured headings | "Baseado no diagnostico, identificamos tres restricoes primarias que explicam 78% da variacao de receita mensal." |
| Sales meetings | Consultive, question-led, direct | Open with a diagnostic question — never with a pitch | "Me diga uma coisa: quanto da sua receita do ultimo trimestre voce conseguiu prever com confianca no inicio do periodo?" |
| Email | Direct, value-dense, one-CTA | No preamble. No pleasantries beyond the minimum. | Lead immediately with the reason for the email. |
| Presentations | Structured, data-grounded, decisive | Use architecture metaphors, commercial metrics, phase frameworks | Build from diagnosis to prescription — never reverse. |

## Vocabulary

### USE — Words and Phrases

Architectural and commercial vocabulary. These are ZNITH's native language:

- Estrutura, arquitetura, governanca, previsibilidade
- Diagnostico, restricao real, gargalo estrutural
- Receita, pipeline, conversao, resultado comercial
- Processo, playbook, cadencia, fluxo
- Arquitetura comercial, operacao comercial, sistema comercial
- "Na pratica" (not "em termos praticos")
- "Funciona assim:" (not "o processo se da da seguinte maneira")
- "O que trava e..." (diagnosis language)
- Specific numbers, timeframes, commercial outcomes
- Maquina de vendas previsivel (Leilaine's coined term — usable in institutional contexts)
- Cemiterio de ferramentas (when naming the problem clients arrive with)
- Diagnostico comercial (always use this term, not "auditoria" or "avaliacao")
- Legado com estrutura (when speaking about permanence and impact)
- Setup e implantacao (for the AI product specifically)
- Acompanhamento de governanca (for ongoing services)
- Investimento em arquitetura (never "custo")
- Restricao comercial, gargalo de crescimento

### NEVER USE — Prohibited Words and Phrases

Using any of these breaks the brand voice immediately:

- Revolucionario, disruptivo, inovador, cutting-edge, vanguarda, game-changer
- Sinergia, ecossistema (in vague contexts), stakeholders (without specificity)
- Alavancar, impulsionar, potencializar, transformar (in the hype sense)
- Hype de IA of any kind: "o futuro e agora," "a revolucao da IA," "o mundo mudou"
- Academic transitions: "no que tange," "destarte," "outrossim," "mediante o exposto," "nesse sentido"
- Vague promises: "resultados incriveis," "sucesso garantido," "transforme sua empresa"
- Motivational fluff: "voce pode!", "acredite em voce," "sonhe grande," "desperte seu potencial"
- Guru positioning: "metodo definitivo," "o sistema que ninguem te contou," "o segredo que os grandes..."
- SaaS/pricing language: "plano," "pacote," "tier," "premium," "starter," "enterprise" as tier names
- "Custo" when referring to ZNITH's services (use "investimento")
- "Chatbot" (use "agente" or "ZNITH.AI Agente")
- "Coaching" (use "mentoria estrategica")
- "Consultor" for team members (use "Arquiteto Comercial")
- "Instalacao" for the AI product (use "setup e implantacao")
- "Monthly retainer" (use "RCaaS" or "acompanhamento de governanca")
- Adjectives without proof: "o melhor," "lider," "incomparavel," "incrivel"

## PT-BR Tone Rules

Portuguese-specific calibration for Brazilian executive communication:

1. **"Olha" at sentence starts** — permitted sparingly when grounding a point: "Olha, a maioria das empresas que nos procura ja tentou AI antes."
2. **"Na pratica"** — preferred over "em termos praticos" or "na pratica isso significa que"
3. **Short declaratives as punches** — end a paragraph with a single-sentence verdict. One word can be a sentence: "Estrutura primeiro. Velocidade depois."
4. **No academic register** — zero "conforme," "mediante," "cabe ressaltar," "vale destacar"
5. **Contractions of address** — use "voce" not "o senhor/a senhora" in all digital contexts
6. **No hashtag language** in written copy — even in LinkedIn institutional posts, avoid using cultural slang as a hook
7. **Numbers are your allies** — specific numbers always outperform adjectives: "R$2.3M em receita atribuida a AI em 6 meses" beats "resultados expressivos"
8. **Rhythm variation** — short sentence. Then a longer sentence that builds the argument with context and specifics. Then a short one that lands the conclusion. Never three similar-length sentences in a row.

## Anti-Patterns

What the ZNITH company voice would NEVER sound like:

1. **The AI hype voice**: "A inteligencia artificial esta revolucionando o mundo dos negocios. As empresas que nao se adaptarem ficarao para tras." — This sounds like every generic AI company. ZNITH names the specific problem and the specific structure.

2. **The motivational coach voice**: "Sua empresa tem potencial incrivel! Voce pode ir muito alem dos seus limites atuais!" — ZNITH speaks from diagnosis, not encouragement. Never affirms without evidence.

3. **The feature-listing SaaS voice**: "Nossa plataforma oferece CRM integrado, automacao de leads, dashboard em tempo real e muito mais!" — ZNITH speaks about commercial outcomes, not tool features.

4. **The false-modesty voice**: "Nos somos apenas uma empresa que tenta ajudar os empresarios a crescerem..." — ZNITH has 20+ years and R$500M+ in influenced sales. It speaks from authority, not false humility.

5. **The urgent-scarcity voice**: "Vagas limitadas! Aproveite agora antes que seja tarde!" — ZNITH's scarcity is real and structural (limited capacity by design), never manipulative urgency.

6. **The over-explaining voice**: Long paragraphs that hedge every statement with qualifications: "It might be the case that, depending on your specific situation, there could potentially be some areas where..." — ZNITH is decisive. If it sees a problem, it names it.

7. **The testimonial-dependent voice**: Leading with "Our clients say..." before establishing the argument. ZNITH's authority comes from demonstrated reasoning, not social proof as the primary argument.
