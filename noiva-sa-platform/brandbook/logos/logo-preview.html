Design Rationale

  • Variation A (Classic Dual Tone): Simplest read. Two circles, two colors. The overlap is naturally visible through layering. Best for large applications.
  • Variation B (Overlap Reveal): Uses clipping to create a true interlock effect where the left ring passes "in front of" the right ring in the overlap zone. Creates visual depth and the sense of two physical rings linked together. Recommended primary version.
  • Variation C (Monoline Rose): Single-color version for contexts where dual-tone is too complex (e.g. embossing, single-color print). Elegant simplicity.
  • Overlap distance is 28px (center-to-center = 28px vs radius 18px each), creating a ~8px overlap zone. This is roughly the golden ratio proportion of the circle diameter.
  • At small sizes (32px and below), stroke weight increases to 2.5-4px to maintain visibility. The interlock effect remains readable down to 32px.

Regras de Uso

Logo Usage Guidelines

Correct

  • Use on approved backgrounds only
  • Maintain clear space = height of "S"
  • Use provided color versions
  • Scale proportionally
  • Minimum size: 32px for symbol

Never

  • Distort, rotate, or skew
  • Add drop shadows or effects
  • Change the colors arbitrarily
  • Place on busy photography
  • Use botanical motifs touching logo
