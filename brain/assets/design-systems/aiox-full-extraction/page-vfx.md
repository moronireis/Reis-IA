## Key Design Decisions
- Film grain adds analog texture to digital surfaces
- Blend modes for creative compositing
- Blur effects for glass morphism and depth
- Glow effects use lime neon tokens for brand consistency
- CRT/scanlines reinforce the "dark cockpit" aesthetic
