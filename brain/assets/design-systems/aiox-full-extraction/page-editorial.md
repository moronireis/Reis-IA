# AIOX — Editorial Page
URL: https://brand.aioxsquad.ai/brandbook/editorial

## Page Purpose
Editorial spreads documenting brand storytelling through 26 numbered layouts. Visual reference for brand communication materials.

## Content Structure
26 editorial spreads with large-format typography, split-screen layouts, and brand narrative content.

## Design Patterns
- **Large numeric anchors**: 288pt to 36pt cascading type scale
- **50/50 split-screen layouts**: Dark/cream backgrounds with 1px dividers
- **Corner ornaments**: 20px crosshair graphics in lime
- **Color swatches**: Inline hex/token references
- **4-act hero narrative framework**

## Typography
- TASA Orbiter Display (primary headlines)
- Geist (secondary body)
- Roboto Mono (code/technical)

## Brand Vocabulary Rules
**Mandatory phrases**: "Agora o controle e seu", "X marks the spot", "Hackear"
**Prohibited**: Disruptive, revolutionary, magical, easy/simple, em-dashes
