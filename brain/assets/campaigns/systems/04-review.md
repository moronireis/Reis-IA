### Pass 1: Brand Voice Audit

**Score: 9/10**

The copy nails the consultive premium tone. It reads like a strategic partner addressing a peer executive -- not an agency pitching services. This is the most confident of all Reis IA pages, and it earns that distinction.

**What works:**
- Porsche-level understated confidence. The copy does not sell -- it states. "Voce nao precisa entender de AI. Precisa de alguem que entenda -- e que comece pela receita." This is the tone of someone who does not need your business but knows you need theirs.
- PT-BR is natural and executive-grade. Liberal use of pra/pro contractions (40+ instances) creates oral-formal register without sounding casual. No academicismo anywhere.
- C-level appropriate throughout. Language targets CEO/COO R$5-100M. Zero jargon tech without business context. Every AI claim is tied to a specific business outcome or number.
- No AI hype. Zero instances of "revolucionario", "disruptivo", "o futuro e agora". Every mention of AI is instrumentalized -- it is a tool for revenue, not a marvel.
- Identity test passes. If you covered the name, the Revenue-First Framework, Cemiterio de Prototipos, the Audit mechanism, and the confident peer-to-peer register would identify this as Reis IA.

**Minor issue:**
- D13, line 460: "A pergunta central continua a mesma" is slightly generic. Could be sharpened to something more distinctive. Not a blocker.

### Strengths

1. **Tone calibration is exceptional.** The most confident of all Reis IA pages. Zero hedging, zero softening. Reads like a senior partner at a top consultancy who happens to build AI systems. The Porsche comparison is earned.

2. **Cemiterio de Prototipos framing is masterful.** The enemy is always the vendor/methodology, never the reader. "Isso nao foi azar. Foi ausencia de metodo." -- this single line validates the reader's experience and repositions the failure as systemic, not personal. The reader is freed to try again.

3. **Revenue-First Framework positioning is strong and differentiated.** Introduced as the methodological answer to the Prototype Graveyard, not a marketing gimmick. "A diferenca entre um sistema que da resultado e um prototipo caro? A ordem das perguntas." -- clean, memorable, specific. The reader can repeat this to their board.

4. **AI Revenue Audit section (D6) is the best section.** It clearly communicates standalone value, details exactly what the reader receives, and positions the Audit as a decision-making tool, not a sales trap. "Mesmo que voce nunca contrate a Reis IA, sai do Audit sabendo..." -- this line will convert skeptics.

5. **Guarantee copy doubles as methodology proof.** "Isso nao e marketing. E o que acontece quando voce comeca todo projeto com uma pergunta: 'O que exatamente define sucesso aqui?'" -- the guarantee section doesn't just reverse risk; it proves the methodology is real.

6. **Problem/Agitation (D5) is textbook Hormozi cost-stacking.** Three scenarios (vendas 70%, suporte 60-80%, processos) create accumulating urgency. The specific numbers (R$44.800/month in Email 3, R$11.550/month) make the reader do the math and feel the pain.

7. **Builder/Systems separation is complete.** Not a single word from the Builder lexicon appears. The tone, vocabulary, and framing are so different these could be from different companies. The ICPs are fundamentally different people, and the copy treats them that way.

8. **Email sequence is well-structured.** Three distinct angles (enemy, mechanism, urgency+guarantee), escalating CTA intensity, standalone readability. Email 3's cost-calculation scenarios with specific R$ figures are particularly strong.

9. **Zero prohibited words in copy body.** Full compliance with humanization rules. Every conjugation of transformar, alavancar, potencializar, impulsionar scanned and confirmed absent.

10. **Humanization is appropriately subtle for Systems ICP.** The humanizer's strategic decision to suppress casual signals (zero "Olha" openers, zero asides, zero self-corrections) is correct. The pra/pro contractions and strategic fragments create natural flow without undermining executive authority.
