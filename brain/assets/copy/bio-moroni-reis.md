### Full Bio — Moroni Reis

Moroni Reis is the founder and CEO of Reis IA, an AI Revenue Ecosystem that helps businesses and digital agencies move beyond AI prototypes and into AI systems that generate measurable revenue. He created Reis IA after more than a decade at the intersection of digital marketing strategy and business growth — running launches, structuring client acquisition systems, and building performance-driven funnels for companies across Brazil and Latin America.

The founding insight behind Reis IA came from a pattern Moroni kept seeing: companies were spending significant money on AI with almost nothing to show for it. Not because the technology was broken, but because everyone was starting with the wrong question. "What AI should we build?" instead of "Where does AI make us money?" That inversion — starting with the technology instead of the business outcome — is what Moroni diagnosed as the core failure point of the AI industry. It became the foundation of his Revenue-First AI Framework, the methodology that drives every engagement at Reis IA.

Reis IA operates as a four-pillar ecosystem: Builder (a mentorship program for agencies and entrepreneurs ready to create and sell AI services), Systems (done-for-you AI implementation for growth-stage companies), Partners (a revenue-share model allowing agencies to sell AI projects without hiring engineers), and Network (an education and collaboration community for AI practitioners and entrepreneurs). The ecosystem model is intentional — Moroni's view is that implementation alone doesn't scale, education alone doesn't produce results, and partnership alone doesn't build capability. The companies and agencies that win with AI need all three, connected under one relationship.

Moroni brings a distinctive perspective shaped by years operating in the Brazilian market while building toward a global audience. He approaches AI not as a technologist but as a business strategist — one who uses data, frameworks, and market research to make decisions, and communicates them with the directness and specificity that business owners need to act. His content, programs, and client work consistently emphasize practical application over theory, revenue impact over feature lists, and systems over shortcuts.

He is the author of the Revenue-First AI Framework, host of the Reis IA Network community, and has been building AI revenue systems for agencies and companies since 2024. He is currently focused on helping the first generation of AI-enabled agencies in the Brazilian and Latin American markets establish competitive advantages before the market consolidates.
