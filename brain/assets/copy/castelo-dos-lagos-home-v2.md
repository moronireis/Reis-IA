## 0. Strategic context (why this rewrite exists)

The previous home draft leaned fully into Aman/Belmond editorial poetry and broke on three counts:

1. **Fabrications**: "Suítes" (the estate has no lodging), "4 lagos" (unverified count), "1890" founding year (unverified), "four generations" (unverified). A premium site that invents facts stops being premium the moment one fact is questioned.
2. **Zero utility**: a real bride in Ribeirão Pires needs to know *what the space offers, what events it hosts, how to visit, and who vouches for it.* The old draft gave her seven photographs and a feeling. Feeling without function converts nothing.
3. **Zero local SEO**: "Ribeirão Pires" never appeared. The client's current site ranks partly on that exact phrase. Throwing it away is donating traffic to competitors.

This v2 keeps every win of the editorial system (single-sentence serif heroes, Roman numeral chapters, section-as-page, no wedding-industry vocabulary, no pricing, no testimonial carousels) and grafts onto it the minimum functional scaffolding a working venue site needs: **infrastructure, event types, parceiros as social proof, and a real contact block**.

The posture is unchanged: the house is speaking. The family runs the estate. Weddings are a consequence, not a product. What changes is that the house is now also willing to tell you the salon's dimensions and where the gate is.
