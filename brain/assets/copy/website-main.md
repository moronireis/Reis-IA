## COPY NOTES FOR DESIGN TEAM

- Hero headline designed for large type — two short lines, maximum contrast
- Manifesto section: let the copy breathe. Generous line height. No more than 60 characters per line.
- Ecosystem cards: headline + body + 3-4 bullet points + CTA. Strict discipline on word count.
- Demo section: copy is minimal — let the demo do the work. Labels and short descriptions only.
- Proof blocks: lead with the metric, then the story. Numbers in large type.
- Footer CTA: full-width dark section. Maximum urgency without manufactured pressure.
