## STRATEGIC INTENT

Highlights are not casual content storage. They are positioning assets — a curated experience that a new profile visitor encounters before they follow, before they DM, and before they buy. Every highlight exists to accomplish one of three jobs:

1. Build immediate credibility (who is this person / brand and why should I trust them)
2. Educate on the core belief and framework (Revenue-First AI)
3. Route the visitor toward the next step (book a call, apply, follow)

Both profiles below serve these jobs — but with different angles, audiences, and tones.

## HIGHLIGHT PRODUCTION NOTES

**Design direction for both profiles:**
- Black background, white text, minimal graphic elements
- Highlight covers: single icon or word, high contrast, premium aesthetic
- No busy graphics or gradients — flat, clean, typographic
- Font: modern sans-serif (Inter, Neue Haas Grotesk, or similar)
- Tone of photography: confident, working, real environments — not stock or overly polished

**Content update cadence:**
- Review and update highlights monthly
- Replace placeholder case studies as real results become available
- Rotate "Behind the Build" content quarterly to reflect current stage
- Keep CTAs current — dead links kill credibility
