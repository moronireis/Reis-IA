## SECTION 1 — PROBLEM

**Section header (internal):** [PROBLEM]

---

### "You've Seen What AI Can Do. You've Also Seen What Happens When It Doesn't Work."

The headlines are clear.

Companies that implement AI correctly are reducing support costs by 30–40%. They're qualifying more leads without hiring more salespeople. They're scaling operations without scaling headcount.

And then there's the other story — the one that doesn't make the case studies.

The chatbot that got built, launched, and forgotten within six months. The AI initiative that started with a vendor demo and ended with a project in limbo. The automation that worked in testing and broke in production.

**42% of companies abandoned AI projects in 2025.**

Not because AI doesn't work. Because the approach was wrong.

Most AI projects start with a technology decision — "let's build a chatbot," "let's automate this workflow," "let's implement an AI agent." Then they get built. And then the business waits for results that don't materialize, because nobody mapped where the results were supposed to come from.

That's the Prototype Graveyard. And it's where most AI investment ends up.

---

**The symptoms look like this:**

- You had an AI project scoped, built, and launched — and nobody's measuring what it actually does for the business
- Your team is still doing the same manual work they were doing before "AI was implemented"
- You got a demo that looked impressive and a deliverable that doesn't match
- You tried no-code tools or freelancers and hit the ceiling of what they can actually build
- You've been told your data isn't clean enough, your systems are too complex, or your use case is too custom — and you're stuck

Or you haven't started at all, because every vendor pitch you've seen led with technology capabilities you couldn't evaluate, not business outcomes you could measure.

## SECTION 5 — HOW IT WORKS

**Section header (internal):** [PROCESS]

---

### How a Reis IA Systems Project Works

**Step 1: AI Revenue Audit (Week 1)**
A 60–90 minute session where we map your business, your tech stack, and your operations against the Revenue-First framework. You'll receive a written report with:
- Top 3–5 AI revenue opportunities in your business
- Priority ranking by effort vs. impact
- Budget and timeline estimates for each recommendation
- Integration requirements for your existing systems

This report is valuable regardless of next steps — you'll know exactly where AI makes money in your business before spending another dollar.

**Step 2: Implementation Plan (Week 1–2)**
Based on the audit, we define the scope, timeline, KPIs, and integration requirements for the implementation. No ambiguity. No "we'll figure it out as we go." Every implementation starts with a clear definition of what success looks like.

**Step 3: Build (Weeks 2–6)**
We build the agreed systems. You receive progress updates throughout. No black box — you see what's being built and why.

**Step 4: Integration and Testing (Weeks 4–7)**
We integrate with your existing systems — CRM, WhatsApp, website, APIs — and run structured testing before launch. Your team is involved in testing so they understand what's been built.

**Step 5: Launch and Optimization (Weeks 6–8+)**
Systems launch into production. We monitor for the first 4 weeks and optimize based on real usage data. We don't disappear at launch.

**Step 6: Team Training and Handoff**
Your team is trained on how the systems work, how to maintain them, and when to escalate issues. You receive full documentation. You own everything — code, documentation, access credentials, all of it.

**Step 7: 60-Day Performance Window**
After handoff, we monitor KPIs for 60 days. If agreed targets aren't trending in the right direction, we continue optimizing at no additional cost.

## SECTION 10 — FAQ

**Section header (internal):** [FAQ]

---

**Q: What if we tried AI before and it didn't work?**

A: That's actually the most common thing we hear. The reason almost every AI failure we've seen comes down to one thing: the project started with the technology instead of the business outcome. Our process starts with the AI Revenue Audit — which maps where AI creates measurable revenue impact before we build anything. That's the difference.

---

**Q: Will this integrate with our existing systems?**

A: Integration is the first thing we map in the AI Revenue Audit. We work with WhatsApp Business API, HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, custom CRMs, ERPs, and most systems with an API. Before any implementation starts, you'll have a clear picture of every integration point and any constraints.

---

**Q: How long before we see results?**

A: The Starter Package is designed for a 2–4 week implementation with results visible within 30 days of launch. Full implementations typically take 4–8 weeks to build and integrate, with optimized results visible within 60 days of launch. We target the highest-impact use cases first specifically to produce early returns.

---

**Q: What happens to our data? Is it secure?**

A: Your data stays on your systems or approved cloud infrastructure — never on Reis IA's servers. We follow data minimization principles: the AI accesses only what it needs to function. For regulated industries (LGPD, GDPR, HIPAA), we design implementations that meet your compliance requirements. This is addressed explicitly in the AI Revenue Audit.

---

**Q: What if something breaks after handoff?**

A: You receive full documentation, credentials, and access to everything we build — you own it completely. The Starter Package includes 30 days of post-handoff support. Full implementations include 60 days of priority support and a 60-day performance guarantee. We also offer monthly retainers for ongoing management.

---

**Q: Do we need clean data to start?**

A: Not necessarily. Most companies start with imperfect data, and the audit process identifies what data requirements exist for each specific implementation. Many AI implementations don't require large datasets — they require clear business logic, which we help you define.

---

**Q: Can we scale after the initial implementation?**

A: Yes — and this is by design. Every Reis IA Systems implementation is built for scalability from the start. We document everything so future additions are straightforward, and the Full Implementation tier is specifically designed for multi-system deployments that grow with your business.
