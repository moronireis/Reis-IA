## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATION: WHICH LEAD MAGNET TO LEAD WITH

**Recommendation: "The AI Agency Profit Calculator"**

**Rationale:**
- Primary ICP (agencies) is solution-aware — they know they need AI, they're evaluating how
- An interactive calculator has higher perceived value than a PDF guide
- The output is personalized (their specific numbers) — creates a stronger emotional pull
- It naturally surfaces the revenue opportunity, which positions the Builder program perfectly
- It's shareable (agencies might forward to a business partner before signing up themselves)
- The data collected (agency size, current revenue, services) pre-qualifies leads for the sales call

**What the calculator shows:**
- Current agency revenue benchmark vs. AI-enabled agencies in their space
- Estimated additional annual revenue from adding one AI service line
- Estimated average ticket size for AI projects in their service category
- A simple ROI projection: "If you add $X/month in AI revenue, in 12 months you'll have earned $Y"

**Alternative lead magnets (secondary / future):**
- "The AI Offer Blueprint" — PDF guide for structuring an AI service offer (lower friction, lower conversion quality)
- "5 AI Services Any Agency Can Sell This Month" — quick-win content guide (high traffic, lower intent)

**For the Systems ICP (B2B companies), the primary lead magnet is the free AI Revenue Assessment** — a live call, not a download. The landing page copy for that is embedded in the sales-page-systems.md file.

## PAGE STRUCTURE / DESIGN NOTES

- Above the fold: headline, subheadline, single CTA. Nothing else.
- Calculator itself should be an embedded tool on the page — not a download
- Form should appear inline (not a popup) — reduces friction
- Results appear on the same page below the fold (or on a next-step page)
- Mobile-first: all form elements large enough to tap without zooming
- Trust signals: add Moroni's photo + short 1-line bio near the opt-in form
- No navigation on this page — it's a conversion page, not an information page
