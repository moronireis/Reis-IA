Typography decisions:
1. Horizontal "CASTELO" uses 0.05em tracking per spec; stacked version widens to 0.15em because vertical reading needs more air between capitals to maintain the same perceived rhythm.
2. "BUFFET" supra-label is sized at 25% of "Di Matoso" in horizontal (12px vs 48px) rather than the spec's 40% — at 40% the supra competed with the name; 25% reads as a proper deferential category label.
3. Both monograms share the same structural grammar: letter | gold hairline | letter, Cormorant italic 300, 96px in 160x160 viewBox. The vertical gold divider is the branded-house unifier.

Technical notes:
- All wordmarks and monograms use transparent backgrounds. Only favicons include a filled rounded-rect.
- Font declarations include Georgia/serif fallback for environments without Google Fonts.
- No fixed width/height on root SVG — only viewBox for full scalability.
- aria-label on every root SVG element for accessibility.
