## 1. Current Funnel Diagnosis (BROKEN)

Current flow: Ad → WhatsApp → Price PDF → 1.5h Meeting (Giovana) → Follow-up → Close

### Structural Problems Identified:
1. **Price before value** — PDF with prices kills curiosity before delivering value
2. **Giovana IS the funnel** — she's the seller, scheduler, deliverer. Overload = lost conversion
3. **No positioning** — brides arrive cold, no authority context, no social proof
4. **Expectation of Giovana personally** — business can't scale if tied to one person
5. **No structured post-sale** — missed referral and retention opportunities

## 5. Sales Process Redesign

### Flow:
1. Lead arrives (quiz, ad, DM)
2. WhatsApp bot: 3 qualification questions
3. If unqualified → nurture sequence (welcome, value, social proof, invitation)
4. If qualified → meeting booking (automatic scheduling)
5. Saleswoman meeting (45 min, scripted)
6. Personalized proposal presented during meeting
7. Follow-up with deadline

### Script Structure:
- Conexão (rapport building)
- Diagnóstico (understanding their specific situation)
- Método (presenting the Noiva S.A. method)
- Pacotes (presenting the right package based on qualification data)

### Key: Bride already informed budget via quiz BEFORE meeting
- Saleswoman already knows what package fits
- No surprises on price
- Proposal adapted to their stated budget and needs

### Objections to Map:
1. "É caro" (It's expensive)
2. "Assessoria do buffet já faz" (Venue's coordinator already does this)
3. "Preciso pensar" (I need to think)
4. "Noivo acha caro" (Groom thinks it's expensive)

### Tools:
- **Fathom** for recording all sales calls → transcription → analysis by Moroni
- Sales recordings feed into script optimization

## 6. Product Definitions (Updated from Meeting)

### Assessoria Início (Entry — ~R$3,100)
- 60 days before wedding
- Execution only
- No ongoing assessora contact
- Equivalent to "Assessoria Final/do Dia" in market terms

### Assessoria Completa (Main — ~R$4,500)
- Also starts 60 days before
- Gets: system access, briefing, vendor recommendations
- Gets: Clube de Fornecedores, community access
- **NO physical assessora** (NO person dedicated)
- Noiva S.A. as company helps, not a specific person
- NOTE: "Completa" naming creates expectation issue — brides think "completa" = assessora from day 1. Need to manage this in copy.

### Assessoria Exclusiva/Master (Premium — ~R$7,500)
- Assessora from contract signing day
- WhatsApp direct contact with assessora
- Wedding date/venue status irrelevant
- Full hands-on service

### NEW: Giovana Direct (Future tier)
- Higher ticket than Exclusiva
- Giovana personally as assessora
- Separated from standard Noiva S.A. packages
- NOT sold through standard funnel — special request only

### CRITICAL: Remove "WhatsApp direto com a Giovana" from all existing proposals
- Giovana = face of company, but NOT the assessora in standard packages
- Only in the future Giovana Direct tier
