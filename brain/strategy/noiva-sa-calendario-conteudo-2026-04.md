## 1. Content Pillars

| Pillar | % | Purpose | Example Topics |
|--------|---|---------|---------------|
| **Metodo** | 30% | Show how Noiva S.A. works weddings | Timeline management, vendor coordination, day-of logistics, checklist review |
| **Bastidor** | 25% | Behind-the-scenes, problem-solving | Zipper broke on dress (real story), vendor no-show fix, rain plan activation |
| **Educacao** | 25% | Teach brides wedding planning | How to combine outfit+shoes, venue comparison, budget tips, contract red flags |
| **Prova Social** | 20% | Testimonials, wedding footage | Couple stories, day-of footage, before/after reveals, bride reactions |

### Growth Content (cross-cuts all pillars):
- **Collabs com fornecedores** — dual Instagram posting, relationship building, future club pipeline
- **Conteudo viral/meme** — always branded (Noiva S.A. colors, style), like CIMED approach
- **Inspiracao/dicas** — what brides save most (pista animada, trend de foto, referências)

### Dual-Angle Technique:
One filming session can produce 2 contents:
- **Version A (Bastidor):** Giovana's POV as assessora solving the problem
- **Version B (Prova Social):** Bride's POV showing the result/testimonial

## 4. Content Formats by Platform

### Instagram Reels (Primary)
- 15-60 seconds
- Hook nos primeiros 3 segundos
- Texto na tela (legendas)
- CTA no final ou nos comentarios
- Hashtags relevantes (casamento, noiva, assessoria)

### Instagram Stories
- Behind-the-scenes casual
- Enquetes e caixinhas de perguntas
- Compartilhamento de Reels
- Bastidores de eventos
- Dicas rapidas

### TikTok (Secondary)
- Mesmo conteudo do Reels adaptado
- Trends e audios populares
- Mais informal/divertido

### Instagram Carrossel (2x/semana)
- Educativo/informativo
- "5 coisas que...", "Guia de...", "Comparativo..."
- Salvaveis e compartilhaveis

## 5. Collab Strategy with Vendors

### Objectives:
1. **Growth** — appear on vendor's Instagram, reach their audience
2. **Relationship** — build connections for future vendor club
3. **Content** — joint content is naturally more engaging
4. **Long-term** — when Noiva S.A. has large following, sell ad space

### Collab Formats:
| Format | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Bastidor + Fornecedor** | Show the vendor's work at a wedding |
| **Educacao + Fornecedor** | "Como combinar vestido + sapato" with a dress shop |
| **Prova Social + Fornecedor** | Triple collab: bride testimonial + vendor showcase |
| **Dica + Fornecedor** | "Trend de iluminacao de pista" with lighting vendor |

### Approach:
- Start with vendors Giovana already works with
- Propose mutual benefit (both post as collab)
- Film during actual weddings (authentic content)
- Tag and cross-promote

## 8. Brand Guidelines for Content

### Visual:
- Dark premium aesthetic
- Rose (#DBA99F) and cream (#F4F3EE) accents
- Cormorant Garamond for text overlays (headlines)
- Montserrat for UI elements
- Consistent templates for carousels and stories
- Logo watermark on all produced content

### Voice:
- Acolhedor mas profissional
- Giovana fala como amiga experiente, nao como vendedora
- Humor leve quando cabe (memes, trends)
- Sempre terminar com valor ou CTA claro
- Nunca parecer desesperado por venda

### Prohibited:
- Emojis excessivos
- Linguagem muito informal/gíria
- Promessas absolutas ("casamento perfeito garantido")
- Falar mal de concorrentes
- Compartilhar dados de clientes sem permissao
