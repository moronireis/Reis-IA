## Strategic Thesis

Reis IA reaches 100K in revenue within 90 days by focusing on two high-leverage moves:

1. **Launch the Builder mentorship** with a cohort of 8-12 agency owners ($50K-$85K from one cohort)
2. **Close 3-5 Systems projects** through direct outreach, content, and early Partner referrals ($25K-$60K)

Everything else — community building, content engine, partnerships at scale — supports these two revenue drivers but should not distract from them.

## Channel Strategy

### Tier 1 — Primary Channels (70% of effort)

#### LinkedIn (Organic)
- **Why**: Agency owners and B2B decision-makers live here. High organic reach for AI content. Zero cost.
- **Frequency**: 1 post per day, 5 days per week
- **Content mix**: 40% educational (AI tips, frameworks), 30% storytelling (case studies, behind-the-scenes), 20% opinion/thought leadership, 10% direct offers
- **Engagement**: Comment on 10-15 relevant posts per day, engage in agency groups
- **KPI**: 1,000 followers by day 90, 50+ DM conversations, 10+ inbound inquiries

#### YouTube
- **Why**: Long-form content builds deep trust. Evergreen. Works for both ICP segments.
- **Frequency**: 1 video per week (8-15 minutes)
- **Content**: AI implementation walkthroughs, "How to sell AI as an agency," case studies, market analysis
- **KPI**: 12 videos published by day 90, 500+ subscribers, content repurposed across all channels

#### Direct Outreach (LinkedIn DMs + Email)
- **Why**: At early stage, founder-led outreach is the fastest path to revenue. Zero cost, high conversion.
- **Volume**: 10-15 personalized outreach messages per day
- **Target**: Agency owners showing interest in AI (content engagement, job posts, profile signals)
- **KPI**: 5% reply rate, 2% to sales call, 20-30 calls booked in 90 days

### Tier 2 — Secondary Channels (20% of effort)

#### Meta Ads (Paid)
- **Why**: Retarget warm audiences at low cost. Scale cold traffic once funnel converts organically.
- **Budget**: Start at $500/month (day 30), increase to $1,500-$3,000/month by day 60 if profitable
- **Strategy**: Phase 1 = retargeting only. Phase 2 = lookalike audiences from opt-ins and buyers.
- **KPI**: Under $5 cost per lead (retargeting), under $15 cost per lead (cold)

#### Instagram
- **Why**: Strong for Brazilian market. Reels from YouTube content. Stories for daily engagement.
- **Frequency**: 3-5 reels per week (repurposed from YouTube), daily stories
- **KPI**: Growing follower count, DM conversations from stories

#### Workshops/Webinars
- **Why**: High-conversion events for solution-aware audience. Build authority quickly.
- **Frequency**: 1 per month (minimum)
- **Format**: 60-90 min live workshop, with Builder CTA at the end
- **KPI**: 100+ registrants per workshop by month 3, 40% show rate, 10% to application

### Tier 3 — Activation Channels (10% of effort)

#### Referral Program
- **What**: Builder alumni and Partners refer new clients for a bonus or rev-share
- **When**: Activate at day 45+
- **KPI**: 1-2 referral clients per month by month 3

#### Events (In-Person or Virtual)
- **What**: Host or speak at AI/marketing events
- **When**: 1 event in the first 90 days
- **KPI**: 20+ qualified contacts per event

#### Podcast Guest Appearances
- **What**: Appear on marketing, agency, and business podcasts
- **When**: Pitch 5-10 podcasts starting at day 15
- **KPI**: 3-5 appearances in 90 days

## Partnership Strategy

### Phase 1 (Days 1-30): Informal Partnerships
- Identify agencies in Moroni's existing network who could benefit from AI capabilities
- Offer to deliver 1 AI project for their client at a reduced rate to prove the model
- Document the process and results

### Phase 2 (Days 31-60): Formalize the Partners Program
- Create standard partnership agreement templates
- Define rev-share tiers and white-label pricing
- Create partner onboarding materials and sales enablement kit
- Sign 1-2 agencies into formal Partners agreements

### Phase 3 (Days 61-90): Scale Partnerships
- Builder alumni become natural Partner candidates
- Each Partner agency should deliver 1-3 projects per month
- Begin building case studies from Partner-delivered projects
- Create a Partner community for knowledge sharing and support

### Target Partnership Economics
- Each active Partner generates $5K-$15K/month in implementation work
- Reis IA's share: 60-70% of project revenue (in rev-share model)
- 5 active Partners = $15K-$50K/month in recurring-ish revenue
- This becomes the primary scale engine after the initial 90 days

## Content Strategy Overview

### Content Pillars
1. **AI Opportunity** — Why AI matters for businesses now, market trends, urgency
2. **Revenue-First Framework** — How to approach AI from a business outcome perspective
3. **Implementation Stories** — Case studies, walkthroughs, before/after
4. **Agency Growth** — How agencies can add AI to their services and increase revenue
5. **Behind the Build** — Moroni's journey building Reis IA, transparent founder content

### Content Repurposing Chain
```
YouTube long-form (8-15 min)
    → 3-5 short clips for Instagram Reels
    → 1-2 LinkedIn posts from key insights
    → Email newsletter content
    → WhatsApp broadcast snippets
```

One long-form piece per week generates 10-15 pieces of distributed content.

### Content Calendar Cadence
- **Daily**: 1 LinkedIn post
- **Weekly**: 1 YouTube video, 3-5 Instagram reels, 1 email to list
- **Monthly**: 1 free workshop, 1 case study deep-dive, 1 partner/community update

## Risks and Contingencies

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|--------|------------|
| Builder cohort doesn't fill | Medium | High | Reduce cohort size to 5-6, offer early bird discount, increase outreach volume |
| Systems projects take too long to close | High | Medium | Lead with paid AI Revenue Audit as a low-barrier entry, focus on smaller starter projects |
| Content doesn't gain traction | Medium | Low | This is a long game — double down on direct outreach and workshops in the short term |
| Delivery overwhelms Moroni | Medium | High | Cap Builder at 12 clients, limit Systems to 2 concurrent projects, bring on a junior implementer at day 45 |
| Partner agencies can't sell | Medium | Medium | Provide sales scripts and pitch decks, co-sell the first 2-3 deals with them |
| Low show rate on sales calls | Low | Medium | Add WhatsApp reminder sequence, pre-call video, and post-booking confirmation |
