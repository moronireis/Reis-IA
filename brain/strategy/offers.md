## Offer 1: AI Revenue Audit (Entry Offer)

**Pillar**: Systems (entry) + Network
**ICP**: All segments — primary conversion tool for B2B companies and SMBs
**Price**: $500-$1,500 (or free as lead magnet for high-value prospects)

### What It Is
A structured diagnostic that maps where AI can generate the most revenue in a specific business. Delivered as a 60-90 minute session + written report.

### Deliverable
- Revenue opportunity map: top 3-5 areas where AI creates measurable revenue impact
- Current state assessment: what's working, what's broken, what's missing
- Implementation priority matrix: what to build first based on effort vs. impact
- Budget estimate and timeline for recommended implementations
- Written report (PDF) the prospect can share internally

### Strategic Purpose
- Provides immediate value — even if they don't buy anything else
- Demonstrates expertise and the Revenue-First methodology in action
- Creates a natural bridge to Systems or Builder offers
- Generates case study data even from the audit itself
- At $500-$1,500, can be self-liquidating (paid with ads) or used as a free offer for qualified leads

### Positioning
"Before spending a dollar on AI, know exactly where the money is." This is not a sales pitch disguised as an audit — it is a genuine diagnostic that delivers value regardless of next steps.

## Offer 2: Reis IA Systems — Starter Package

**Pillar**: Systems
**ICP**: Operationally Constrained SMBs, B2B companies testing the waters
**Price**: $2,000-$5,000

### What It Is
A single, focused AI implementation — one agent or automation that solves a specific, high-impact problem. Delivered in 2-4 weeks.

### Typical Implementations
- WhatsApp AI support agent
- Lead qualification bot
- FAQ / knowledge base agent
- Appointment scheduling automation
- Basic CRM integration with AI routing

### Offer Stack
- Discovery session (included)
- Single AI agent or automation, fully implemented
- Integration with one existing system (WhatsApp, CRM, website)
- 2 weeks of monitoring and adjustment post-launch
- Training session for the team (30-60 min)
- 30 days of support after handoff

### Risk Reversal
**"Works or We Fix It" Guarantee**: If the implementation doesn't perform as specified within 30 days, Reis IA will rebuild or adjust at no additional cost. If still unsatisfied, receive a 50% credit toward any future project.

### Unit Economics Estimate
- Delivery cost: $500-$1,500 (Moroni's time + tools)
- Gross margin: 60-75%
- Upsell rate target: 30% to full Systems package within 90 days

## Offer 3: Reis IA Systems — Full Implementation

**Pillar**: Systems
**ICP**: Growth-Stage B2B Companies, larger SMBs
**Price**: $5,000-$20,000 per project

### What It Is
End-to-end AI implementation across multiple business processes. Multiple agents and automations working together as a system. Delivered in 4-8 weeks.

### Typical Implementations
- AI sales agent (lead qualification + follow-up + scheduling)
- AI support system (multi-channel: WhatsApp, website, email)
- Operational automation suite (CRM workflows, data processing, reporting)
- Custom AI integrations with existing tech stack
- Internal knowledge base / AI assistant for team

### Offer Stack
- Full Revenue-First AI Audit (included, worth $1,500)
- Implementation of 2-5 AI agents/automations
- Integration with all relevant existing systems
- Custom prompt engineering and training of AI models
- 4 weeks of post-launch monitoring and optimization
- Team training workshop (2 hours)
- 60 days of priority support
- Monthly performance report for first 3 months
- **Bonus**: Access to Reis IA Network community

### Risk Reversal
**Performance Guarantee**: Implementation includes a 60-day optimization window. If agreed-upon KPIs are not trending in the right direction within 60 days, Reis IA provides additional optimization at no charge until targets are on track or issues a partial refund for the underperforming component.

### Pricing Strategy
- Fixed project pricing (not hourly) — anchored to the business value, not time spent
- For larger projects ($10K+), offer payment in 2-3 installments
- Consider outcome-based pricing add-on for select clients: base fee + performance bonus tied to measurable results (e.g., increase in qualified leads, reduction in support tickets)

### Unit Economics Estimate
- Delivery cost: $1,500-$6,000 (time + tools + potential subcontractors)
- Gross margin: 65-80%
- Average project target: $10,000
- Monthly retainer upsell potential: $1,000-$3,000/month for ongoing management

## Offer 4: Reis IA Builder — Mentorship Program

**Pillar**: Builder
**ICP**: Digital Agencies wanting to sell AI, AI professionals/entrepreneurs
**Price**: $4,000-$15,000

### What It Is
A structured mentorship program that teaches agencies and entrepreneurs how to create, package, price, and sell AI solutions. Not a course — a hands-on program with direct access to Moroni and the Reis IA methodology.

### Program Structure (Recommended: 8-12 weeks)

**Module 1: AI Offer Architecture** (Weeks 1-2)
- Map your market and identify the highest-value AI opportunities
- Structure your AI offer (deliverables, pricing, positioning)
- Create your offer documentation and sales materials

**Module 2: Client Acquisition** (Weeks 3-4)
- Build your outreach and prospecting system
- Create content that attracts AI buyers
- Master the AI sales conversation and objection handling

**Module 3: Implementation Framework** (Weeks 5-8)
- Learn the Revenue-First implementation methodology
- Set up your delivery infrastructure (tools, processes, templates)
- Deliver your first AI project (with Reis IA as backup support)

**Module 4: Scale and Systemize** (Weeks 9-12)
- Systematize delivery for repeatability
- Build a pipeline beyond founder-led sales
- Explore the Partners pathway for ongoing Reis IA collaboration

### Offer Stack
- 8-12 weeks of structured mentorship
- Weekly group calls (or 1:1 for premium tier)
- Access to Reis IA's templates, frameworks, and SOPs
- Direct WhatsApp/Slack access to Moroni for questions
- Implementation support on your first 1-2 client projects
- Access to Reis IA Network community (lifetime)
- **Bonus**: Pre-built proposal templates and pricing calculators
- **Bonus**: Reis IA Partners pathway upon completion

### Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes | Best For |
|------|-------|----------|----------|
| Group | $4,000-$6,000 | Group calls, community, templates | Budget-conscious agencies, early-stage entrepreneurs |
| Premium | $8,000-$12,000 | 1:1 calls, priority support, first project review | Established agencies serious about AI pivot |
| VIP | $12,000-$15,000 | Full 1:1, done-with-you first project, Partners fast-track | Agencies ready to go all-in immediately |

### Risk Reversal
**"Build Your Offer or Your Money Back"**: If you complete all modules and don't have a sellable AI offer by the end of the program, receive a full refund. Completion requirement ensures skin in the game from both sides.

### Unit Economics Estimate
- Group cohort of 8-12 people: $32K-$72K per cohort
- Delivery cost: Moroni's time (~10-15 hours/week for 12 weeks) + tools
- Gross margin: 80-90% (group), 75-85% (1:1)
- **This is the highest-margin offer and the fastest path to 100K**

## Offer 5: Reis IA Partners — Partnership Program

**Pillar**: Partners
**ICP**: Agencies who complete Builder or want AI implementation capacity
**Price**: Revenue share model (no upfront fee)

### What It Is
A partnership where the agency sells AI solutions to their clients, and Reis IA provides the technical implementation backbone. The agency maintains the client relationship. Reis IA provides the delivery muscle.

### Revenue Model Options

| Model | Agency Gets | Reis IA Gets | Best When |
|-------|------------|-------------|-----------|
| Rev-share | 30-40% of project | 60-70% of project | Agency brings clients, Reis IA does most delivery |
| White-label | Sets own price | Fixed implementation fee ($2K-$8K) | Agency has strong brand, wants full control |
| Hybrid | Keeps client retainer | Project fee + small rev-share | Agency manages ongoing, Reis IA builds |

### What the Agency Gets
- Full AI implementation capacity without hiring
- Reis IA's Revenue-First methodology and frameworks
- Sales enablement materials (proposals, case studies, pitch decks)
- Technical support and QA on all implementations
- Access to Reis IA's tool stack and templates
- Ongoing training on new AI capabilities and use cases
- Co-marketing opportunities (optional)

### What Reis IA Gets
- Distribution through the agency's existing client base
- Revenue without direct client acquisition cost
- Case studies and implementation data from diverse industries
- Network effects — each partner agency expands the ecosystem

### Strategic Value
This is the **scale engine** for Reis IA. While Systems and Builder require Moroni's direct involvement, the Partners model creates revenue that grows without proportional time investment. Target: 5-10 active partner agencies within 6 months, each generating $5K-$20K/month in implementation work.

## Competitive Pricing Context

From market research:
- Chatbot/conversational AI: $3K-$30K (Reis IA Systems is competitive)
- Workflow automation: $3K-$15K (Reis IA Systems Starter is at the entry point)
- AI strategy consulting: $25K-$200K (Reis IA Builder is positioned well below this)
- Specialist agencies win 67% implementation success vs. 22% for generalists

Reis IA's pricing is intentionally positioned at the accessible end of the Tier 3 (Niche/Boutique) range ($5K-$150K), matching the current stage and target ICP. As case studies accumulate and the brand strengthens, prices can move up significantly — especially for Systems Full and Builder VIP.
