## Funnel Strategy by Offer

Reis IA operates multiple funnels based on the offer and ICP. At this early stage, the priority is building two primary funnels — one for Builder/Partners (agency ICP) and one for Systems (B2B company ICP). All other funnels are secondary and should be activated only after the primary funnels are converting.

---

## Primary Funnel 1: Builder / Partners — Application Funnel

**Target ICP**: Digital agencies wanting to sell AI
**Offer**: Builder mentorship ($4K-$15K) with Partners pathway
**Funnel Type**: Application funnel (appropriate for high-ticket offers in a solution-aware market)

### Funnel Map

```
TRAFFIC                CAPTURE              NURTURE             CONVERT
   |                      |                    |                   |
LinkedIn content    Landing page +       Email sequence      Application form
YouTube videos      Lead magnet          (5-7 emails)        + Sales call
Instagram reels     "AI Agency           WhatsApp nurture    Case study
Paid ads (Meta)     Profit Calculator"   Free workshop       presentation
Referrals           or "AI Offer         or webinar          Offer + pricing
Events/webinars     Blueprint"           Community access    Risk reversal
                         |                    |                   |
                    Email + WhatsApp     Build trust,        Close on call
                    opt-in               show proof,         (45-60 min)
                                         handle objections
```

### Traffic Sources (Priority Order)
1. **Organic LinkedIn** — Moroni posts daily about AI + business, engages in agency communities
2. **YouTube** — Long-form content showing AI implementation case studies, agency growth strategies
3. **Instagram Reels** — Short clips from YouTube content, behind-the-scenes, results
4. **Paid Meta Ads** — Retargeting warm audiences first, then cold lookalike audiences
5. **Webinars/Workshops** — Monthly free workshop "How to Add AI to Your Agency Offer in 30 Days"
6. **Referrals** — Builder alumni and Partners referring other agencies

### Lead Magnets (Choose 1 to Start)
- **"AI Agency Profit Calculator"** — Interactive tool/spreadsheet showing how much an agency can earn adding AI services. Requires email to access. High perceived value, low friction.
- **"The AI Offer Blueprint"** — PDF guide showing the exact framework for structuring an AI service offer. 5-7 pages, actionable, includes templates.
- **"5 AI Services Any Agency Can Sell This Month"** — Quick-win content that shows it's more accessible than they think.

### Email Nurture Sequence (7 emails over 14 days)

| Day | Email | Purpose |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 0 | Welcome + lead magnet delivery | Deliver value, set expectations |
| 1 | "The AI agency opportunity nobody is talking about" | Paint the vision, establish authority |
| 3 | Case study / proof of concept | Build credibility with specific results |
| 5 | "The 3 mistakes agencies make trying to sell AI" | Address objections through education |
| 7 | "How [Agency X] added $15K/month in AI revenue" | Social proof, desire amplification |
| 10 | "Is your agency ready for AI?" (self-assessment) | Qualify and segment |
| 14 | "Applications for the next Builder cohort are open" | Direct CTA to apply |

### Application and Sales Process
1. Application form (10-15 questions) — qualifies budget, timeline, commitment level
2. Application review by Moroni (1-2 days)
3. Sales call (45-60 min) — discovery, presentation, close
4. Onboarding call (within 48 hours of payment)

### Conversion Benchmarks (Targets)

| Stage | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Traffic to landing page | Click-through rate | 2-4% (paid), 5-10% (organic) |
| Landing page to opt-in | Opt-in rate | 25-40% |
| Opt-in to email open | Open rate | 40-55% |
| Email sequence to application | Application rate | 5-10% of opt-ins |
| Application to call booked | Show rate | 70-80% |
| Call to close | Close rate | 20-35% |
| Average deal size | Revenue per close | $6,000-$10,000 |

### Math to 100K (Builder Funnel Only)
- Need: ~12-15 Builder clients at average $7,000 = ~$100K
- At 25% close rate: need ~50-60 sales calls
- At 75% show rate: need ~70-80 applications
- At 7% application rate: need ~1,000-1,150 opt-ins
- At 30% opt-in rate: need ~3,500-3,800 landing page visitors
- Timeline: achievable in 90 days with consistent content + paid ads

## Primary Funnel 2: Systems — Consultation Funnel

**Target ICP**: Growth-stage B2B companies, SMBs
**Offer**: AI Revenue Audit ($500-$1,500) leading to Systems ($2K-$20K)
**Funnel Type**: Consultation funnel with paid diagnostic as entry

### Funnel Map

```
TRAFFIC                CAPTURE              QUALIFY              CONVERT
   |                      |                    |                   |
LinkedIn content    Landing page         Booking page         AI Revenue Audit
YouTube case        "Free AI Revenue     Intake form          (paid session)
  studies           Assessment" or       Pre-call             Audit report
Google Ads          paid Audit offer     questionnaire        + recommendations
Referrals                |                    |                   |
Partner agencies    Email/WhatsApp       Qualify budget       Proposal for
                    opt-in               and timeline         Systems project
                                                                  |
                                                             Close project
                                                             ($2K-$20K)
```

### Traffic Sources (Priority Order)
1. **Referrals from Partner agencies** — This is the scale channel; as Partners grow, so does this funnel
2. **Organic LinkedIn** — Case studies, before/after results, educational content about AI in business
3. **YouTube** — Detailed walkthroughs of AI implementations (without revealing proprietary methods)
4. **Google Ads** — Target intent-based searches: "AI automation for business," "AI chatbot for [industry]"
5. **Local events and networking** — For Brazilian SMB market specifically

### Lead Capture Options
- **Free version**: "AI Revenue Assessment" — a self-assessment quiz or short consultation that maps opportunities. Captures email + phone, leads to paid Audit.
- **Paid version**: Direct offer of the AI Revenue Audit ($500-$1,500) for companies ready to invest. Skip the free step for solution-aware buyers.

### Conversion Benchmarks (Targets)

| Stage | Metric | Target |
|-------|--------|--------|
| Traffic to landing | CTR | 2-5% |
| Landing to booking | Booking rate | 10-20% |
| Booking to show | Show rate | 75-85% |
| Audit to project proposal | Proposal rate | 60-70% |
| Proposal to close | Close rate | 30-50% |
| Average project size | Revenue per close | $8,000-$12,000 |

### Math (Systems Funnel Contribution to 100K)
- 5-8 Systems projects at average $10K = $50K-$80K
- This funnel will ramp more slowly than Builder (longer sales cycle)
- Partner referrals accelerate this funnel significantly in months 2-3

## Tech Stack Recommendations

### Essential (Start Here)
- **Landing pages**: Carrd ($19/yr) or Systeme.io (free tier) — fast, simple, get pages up in hours
- **Email**: Systeme.io or Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — affordable, automation capable
- **CRM**: HubSpot free tier or Pipedrive ($14/mo) — track leads and deals
- **Scheduling**: Calendly free tier — for booking audit calls and sales calls
- **WhatsApp Business**: For Brazilian market nurture and communication
- **Payment**: Stripe or local Brazilian payment processor

### Growth (Add When Profitable)
- **Paid ads**: Meta Ads Manager + Google Ads
- **Video hosting**: YouTube (free) + Loom for personalized outreach
- **Community**: Discord or Circle.so for Network members
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics + UTM tracking
- **Automation**: n8n or Make.com for internal workflow automation (eat your own cooking)

### Do Not Over-Invest In
- Expensive all-in-one platforms before validating the funnel
- Custom website/design before conversions are proven
- Multiple tools that do the same thing
