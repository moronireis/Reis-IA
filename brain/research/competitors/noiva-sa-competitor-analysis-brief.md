## 3. Research Framework (Per Competitor)

### 3.1 Brand & Positioning
- Tagline / positioning statement
- Brand aesthetic (colors, typography, photography style)
- Key differentiators they claim
- Target audience (luxury, mid-market, budget)

### 3.2 Products & Pricing
- Package names and descriptions
- Pricing (if publicly available)
- What's included in each tier
- Upsells, add-ons, extras
- Guarantees or refund policies

### 3.3 Funnel & Sales Process
- How leads enter (ads, organic, referral)
- Lead magnets (quizzes, PDFs, free consultations)
- WhatsApp automation (if visible)
- Sales call structure (free, paid, duration)
- Follow-up process
- **KEY:** Do any use quiz funnels?

### 3.4 Content Strategy
- Instagram: posting frequency, content types, engagement rate
- TikTok: presence, content style, viral content
- YouTube: any video content (education, vlogs, tutorials)
- Blog: topics, SEO keywords
- Stories: frequency, interaction tools (polls, questions)
- Reels: top performing content themes

### 3.5 Community & Retention
- Facebook groups, WhatsApp groups, membership
- Vendor clubs or directories
- Events (online or in-person)
- Alumni/past-bride engagement
- Referral programs

### 3.6 Digital Presence
- Website: quality, conversion optimization, booking flow
- SEO: what keywords they rank for
- Ads: are they running Meta/Google ads? (use Ad Library)
- Reviews: Google, Facebook, WeddingWire (testimonial strategy)

## 4. Key Insights to Extract

### For Funnel Design:
- Which competitors use lead magnets? What types?
- Is anyone using quiz funnels in the Brazilian wedding market?
- How do competitors handle the "price before value" problem?
- What's the standard sales cycle (inquiry → close)?

### For Content Strategy:
- What content types get the most engagement?
- Are collabs with vendors common?
- What's the posting frequency of successful accounts?
- Are any using AI/automation for content?

### For Product Differentiation:
- Does anyone offer a platform/system like Noiva S.A. plans to?
- Community models — anyone doing this well?
- Vendor club/directory — existing models?
- Low-ticket courses in the wedding space?

### For Positioning:
- How is the market segmented (luxury vs. budget)?
- Where is Noiva S.A.'s sweet spot (mid-premium)?
- Unoccupied positioning angles
- "Ecossistema de casamento com metodo" — is anyone claiming this space?
