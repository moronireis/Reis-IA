## Full prompt

```
Hero portrait of a grounded Brazilian executive in his 30s, clean-shaven, short dark hair, wearing a matte black technical jacket with minimal geometric detailing at the collar, looking slightly off-camera to frame left as if reading something just out of view.
Composition: bust shot, figure occupies right third of frame, massive negative space on left for headline placement, shoulders cropped at lower edge.
Lighting: cinematic single key light from upper right at 40 degrees, deep shadow on the far side of the face so half the face falls into near-black, rim light tracing the jaw and shoulder silhouette separating figure from background, subtle volumetric haze catching the key light.
Palette: pure black background (#000000), skin in cool desaturated tones with natural subsurface scattering, single electric blue (#4A90FF) accent caught in the reflection of his eye and one thread of stitching on the jacket collar.
Texture: 35mm film grain, realistic skin micro-detail, subtle pore texture, slight catchlight in the visible eye, fabric weave visible on the jacket.
Mood: focused, grounded, executive presence without performance, the look of someone mid-thought.
Technical: 8k, shot on ARRI Alexa 65, 85mm prime lens, f/1.8, extreme shallow depth of field with only the eyes in sharp focus, shoulders softly falling out.
Style: Annie Leibovitz editorial portrait meets Apple founder photography. No influencer gloss. No LinkedIn corporate headshot. Cinema, not content.
Negative: no fake smile, no teeth showing, no direct eye contact with camera, no corporate background, no white backdrop, no stock pose, no hands in frame, no clutter, no cartoon, no CGI plastic skin, no makeup sheen, no rainbow, no gold/amber lighting (critical), no business suit, no tie, no chess pieces, no crowns.
```

## Regeneration notes

- Face feels staged → reinforce "candid mid-thought, not posed, eyes focused on something off-frame"
- Skin reads plastic → add "realistic subsurface scattering, visible pores, natural imperfection"
- Lighting too even → reinforce "half face in deep shadow falling to near-black, single key light only"
- Background picks up ambient → add "absolute black background, zero fill, zero rim on background itself"
- Jacket feels generic → add "matte technical fabric, minimal geometric detail, no logos, no branding"
