## Full prompt

```
Hero product shot of a monolithic brushed aluminum hourglass (the H1-B hourglass mark) floating in negative space, slowly rotating on its vertical axis, sand frozen mid-fall inside.
Composition: centered, hourglass occupies 35% of frame, massive negative space above and below, the sand column catches the light as a vertical blue thread.
Lighting: cinematic studio rim lighting, single key light upper-left at 45 degrees, deep shadow falloff into pure black, soft volumetric haze in background, rim light tracing the outer contour.
Palette: pure black background (#000000), hourglass in cold brushed aluminum with micro-scratches visible, single electric blue (#4A90FF) glow emanating from the sand column inside, no other colors.
Texture: subtle film grain across entire frame, slight chromatic aberration on specular highlights at the aluminum edges, sand grains rendered individually.
Mood: silent, monumental, the stillness before inevitability.
Technical: 8k, shot on ARRI Alexa 65, 85mm prime, f/2.8, shallow depth of field with only the hourglass in sharp focus, background fades to pure black.
Style: Apple product photography meets Porsche brand film meets 2001 A Space Odyssey monolith. Dark minimalist. Architectural precision. Editorial.
Negative: no stock photography, no lifestyle context, no office, no hands holding it, no people, no gold/amber/orange tones (critical), no rainbow, no cartoon, no gamified 3D, no wooden frame, no ornate base, no sand texture that looks like CGI default, no chess pieces, no crowns.
```

## Regeneration notes

If the first output misses:
- Sand color drifts warm → reinforce "electric blue sand, no warm tones, pure #4A90FF"
- Aluminum reads plastic → add "anodized aluminum, machined surface, visible tool marks"
- Background not black → add "absolute black background, #000000, no gradient, no ambient fill"
- Too ornate → add "minimalist geometric form, no decorative elements, pure silhouette"
