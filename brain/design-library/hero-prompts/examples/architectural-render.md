# Example: Architectural Render — "O TEMPO É REI" Facade

**Template**: B (Architectural Render)
**Intended page**: reis-ia-website.com — philosophy / manifesto section
**Section**: Full-bleed between fold 2 and 3
**Headline context**: "O TEMPO É REI" embossed into a brutalist concrete facade

---

## Full prompt

```
Architectural render of a brutalist concrete monolith at deep blue hour, the headline text "O TEMPO E REI" cast in 3-meter-tall embossed letterforms across the facade, set deep into the concrete, catching a single rim of light.
Composition: wide shot, structure fills lower 60% of frame, infinite black sky above, extreme one-point perspective with leading lines converging off-center right.
Lighting: single low-angle rim light from horizon at frame right, deep shadow falloff into absolute black on the left of the structure, atmospheric haze between camera and facade, no fill light, no bounce.
Palette: pure black sky (#000000), matte concrete in cool grey with subtle blue undertone, electric blue (#4A90FF) only in the rim light reflection on the right edge of the embossed letters.
Texture: 35mm film grain, slight lens distortion on frame edges, shot-on-celluloid imperfection, concrete aggregate visible in close detail.
Mood: monolithic, quiet, inevitable, the weight of time as physical mass.
Technical: 8k, architectural photography, Canon TS-E 17mm tilt-shift, f/8, deep focus, long exposure feel.
Style: Tadao Ando meets Blade Runner 2049 color grading meets the opening shot of Arrival. Editorial architectural. No postcard composition.
Negative: no people, no cars, no trees, no sky color (keep pure black), no stars, no moon, no warm tones (critical), no sunset, no clutter, no signage other than the headline, no decorative elements, no chess pieces, no crowns, no gold/amber.
```

## Regeneration notes

- Sky drifts toward navy → reinforce "pure absolute black sky, zero stars, zero ambient, #000000"
- Concrete reads too warm → add "cool grey concrete with cyan undertone, no warm cast"
- Typography reads as signage → add "embossed into the concrete itself, same material, only visible because of rim light"
- Composition too symmetrical → add "off-center one-point perspective, headline lower-left to upper-right diagonal"
